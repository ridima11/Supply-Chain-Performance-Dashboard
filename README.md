# Supply-Chain-Performance-Dashboard
A business intelligence dashboard analyzing revenue, operations, and product profitability using Power BI and the DataCo Supply Chain Dataset.
## Project Overview
This project is a multi-page Business Intelligence dashboard built in **Power BI** using the **DataCo Supply Chain Dataset**, containing over **180,000 supply chain records**. It brings together sales, operations, logistics, and product performance into a single executive reporting solution designed to support data-driven decision-making.

Rather than treating this as a dashboarding exercise, I approached it as a business reporting project. Every visual was chosen to answer a specific business question, with the goal of helping decision-makers understand not just **what is happening**, but **where they should focus their attention**.
The final report is organized into four pages—**Executive Overview, Sales Analysis, Operations Analysis, and Product Profitability**—each designed for a different business audience while contributing to one cohesive story.

## Business Problem

Supply chain organizations generate large volumes of operational data every day, but raw data alone does not support effective decision-making. Business leaders need a consolidated view of key performance indicators to monitor financial performance, operational efficiency, and product profitability.

The objective of this project was to design a Business Intelligence dashboard that transforms transactional supply chain data into actionable insights. The dashboard enables users to:

- Monitor key business KPIs such as Revenue, Profit, Orders, and Profit Margin.
- Analyze sales performance across markets, countries, products, and customer segments.
- Evaluate delivery performance and shipping efficiency.
- Identify the products and categories that contribute most to profitability.

To address these objectives, the dashboard is organized into four focused report pages, each designed for a specific business function while contributing to an overall view of supply chain performance.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is organized into four report pages, each designed for a different business function. Together, they provide a comprehensive view of supply chain performance, from high-level executive metrics to detailed product profitability.

### Executive Overview

The Executive Overview provides a high-level snapshot of business performance through key financial and operational KPIs. It is designed for stakeholders who need a quick understanding of overall performance before exploring specific business areas.

### Sales Analysis

This page focuses on revenue performance across markets, countries, product categories, and customer segments. It enables users to identify where revenue is generated and compare sales performance across different dimensions.

### Operations Analysis

The Operations Analysis page evaluates delivery performance and shipping efficiency using operational KPIs and logistics metrics. It highlights areas that may require process improvements and helps monitor overall supply chain reliability.

### Product Profitability

This page examines profitability at the department, category, and product levels. Using interactive drill-down analysis, it helps identify the products and categories that contribute most to overall business profit.
